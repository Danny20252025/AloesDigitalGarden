Krokotopia - Pirate101 Gear Guide

How to get to Krokotopia

If you haven't completed the main storyline up to Sinbad Part 3, you must do this first. Once you have done that, go to Madame Vadima, the witchdoctor class trainer. Start her quest, "For Those About Krok". If you have done Krokotopia before, you can return there by going to Flotsam docks in Flotsam Skyway in Skull Island, finding Calico Jake, and boarding his ship.

THE GEAR

The Weapons

  • Sokar's Dagger: Dropped from Sar'Kath. Getting a bonus elusive can be helpful if you have elusive 3.
  • Izft's Blades: Dropped from Sar'Kath.
  • Setesh's Armaments: Dropped from Sar'Kath. The hold the line can help you get hold the line 5 and the riposte isn't bad either. This weapon feels competitive with the best gear in the game.
  • Lunar Anubite Staff: Dropped from Sar'Kath. The 6 range is very good for witchdoctors and the retribution doesn't hurt. Dispel magic can be very good in some situations where you are debuffed. This weapon feels competitive with the best gear.
  • Krokotopian Crossbow: Dropped from Sar'Kath. The 5 range is good. Witch Hunter is a very good epic.
  • Sacred Axe: Dropped from Sar'Kath.

Anubis' Clothes

  • Anubis' Underworld Boots: Dropped from Battle Angel. If you are using a spell-based build, the stats are decent.
  • Anubis' Underworld Garb: Dropped from Sar'Kath and Halls of Justice Draconians. If you are using a spell-based build, the stats are decent. Witch Hunter is a good epic to have.
  • Anubis' Underworld Hat: Dropped from Sar'Kath and Halls of Justice Draconians. The power is a good damage aoe that also slows enemies. It is very good versus melee enemies. The stats are also good for spell builds.

Bellatrix's Clothes

  • Bellatrix's Underworld Hat: Dropped from Tertius Aggredior. The stats are good for swashbuckler. The power is good too.
  • Bellatrix's Underworld Garb: Dropped from Bellatrix and Tertius Aggredior. The stats and epics are really good for shooty/(anymelee) swashbuckler hybrids. It is debatably the best robe piece for swashbucklers.
  • Bellatrix's Underworld Boots: Dropped from Armada Guard. The stats are good for swashbucklers.

Temple Guardian's Clothes

  • Temple Guardian's Boots: Dropped from Bone Drake. The stats are good for buccaneers. The vengeance strike epic can also get you to vengeance strike 5.
  • Temple Guardian's Hat: Dropped from Guardian Scarab. The stats and power are good for buccaneers. The power removes positive buffs from the enemy, which is very helpful in challenging fights.
  • Temple Guardian's Garb: Dropped from Scorpion. This is good for the same reasons as the Temple Guardian's Boots.

Accessories

  • Guardian Scarab's Carapace: Dropped from Guardian Scarab. All three of these epics are amazing on any melee pirate. If you already have higher tiers of turn the tide and elusive, it's even better. It is a competitor with cerberus's collar and probably debatably the best necklace depending on your build.
  • Queen of Battle's Signet: Dropped from Bellatrix. The powers are all decent.

Ammit's Totems

  • Ammit's Totems: All totems are dropped from Spirits of Reprisal. Privateer gets an identical totem to Ammit's Consuming Totem called Ammit's Insatiable Totem. Buccaneer gets an identical totem to Ammit's Voracious Totem called Ammit's Devouring Totem. The krok summon on these totems is pretty good. This totem is competitive with the best gear for Swashbuckler and Buccaneer because second chance is one of the more valuable epics to have.

Where the drops come from

Sar'Kath

  • Location: He is in House of Scales. Flotsam Docks -> Lower Zigazag -> Upper Zigazag -> House of Scales
  • Drops: Sokar's Dagger, Izft's Blades, Setesh's Armamanets, Lunar Anubite Staff, Krokotopian Crossbow, Anubis' Underworld Garb, Anubis' Underworld Hat

Battle Angel

  • Location: They are in Lower Zigazag and in the Armada Ship. Flotsam Docks -> Lower Zigazag -> Armada Ship
  • Drops: Anubis' Underworld Boots

Halls of Justice Draconians

  • Location: They are in the Halls of Justice. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag -> Upper Zigazag -> House of Scales -> Halls of Justice
  • Drops: Anubis' Underworld Garb, Anubis' Underworld Hat

Armada Guard

  • Location: They are in Lower Zigazag. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag
  • Drops: Bellatrix's Underworld Boots

Bellatrix

  • Location: She is in the Command Tent. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag -> Command Tent
  • Drops: Bellatrix's Underworld Garb, Queen of Battle's Signet

Tertius Aggredior

  • Location: They are in Lower Zigazag. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag
  • Drops: Bellatrix's Underworld Garb, Bellatrix's Underworld Hat

Bone Drake

  • Location: They are in Upper Zigazag and House of Scales. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag -> Upper Zigazag -> House of Scales
  • Drops: Temple Guardian's Boots

Scorpion

  • Location: They are in the House of Scales and the Chamber of the Scorpion. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag -> Upper Zigazag -> House of Scales -> Chamber of the Scorpion
  • Drops: Temple Guardian's Garb

Guardian Scarab

  • Location: They are in the Locking Device Chamber. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag -> Upper Zigazag -> House of Scales -> Locking Device Chamber
  • Drops: Temple Guardian's Hat, Guardian Scarab's Carapace

Spirits of Reprisal

  • Location: They are in the House of Scales. Flotsam Skyway -> Lower Zigazag -> Upper Zigazag -> House of Scales
  • Drops: Ammit's Consuming Totem, Ammit's Devouring Totem, Ammit's Famished Totem, Ammit's Insatiable Totem, Ammit's Voracious Totem
